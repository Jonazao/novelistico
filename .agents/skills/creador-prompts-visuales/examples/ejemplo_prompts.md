# Ejemplo de Prompts Visuales Producidos

Este ejemplo ilustra cómo el Skill `creador-prompts-visuales` procesa fichas de personajes y lugares de la Wiki para generar prompts estructurados en inglés.

---

## 1. Ficha del Personaje: Robert Langdon (Con Parche)
*   *Origen del Lore:* `wiki/personajes/robert_langdon.md`

### Prompts Generados:

*   **Prompt 1: Hoja de Modelo (Character Model Sheet)**
    > `Model sheet, concept art character sheet of a 45-year-old intellectual man, tall athletic swimmer build, short brown hair with gray temple streaks, one penetrating blue right eye, a prominent vertical scar over his closed left eyelid, wearing a black leather eyepatch over the left eye. Wearing a classic brown Harris tweed jacket over a gray cashmere sweater vest and dark blue jeans. Full body view, multiple angles showing front, side, and 3/4 views, different facial expressions, solid white background, neutral lighting, game concept art style, highly detailed, Unreal Engine 5 render --ar 16:9`

*   **Prompt 2: Retrato Cinematográfico (Cinematic Portrait)**
    > `Cinematic portrait, close-up shot of a 45-year-old intellectual man with a serious expression, short brown hair with gray streaks, wearing a black leather eyepatch over his left eye, vertical scar visible on his cheek, wearing a tweed jacket. Sitting inside a dimly lit old library, warm light from a desk lamp illuminating half of his face, dramatic shadows, photorealistic, 8k resolution, shot on 85mm lens, moody color grading --ar 16:9`

## 2. Ficha de Lugar: Ensenada (Destruida)
*   *Origen del Lore:* `wiki/lugares/ensenada.md`

### Prompts Generados:

*   **Prompt 1: Toma Panorámica Exterior (Establishing Wide Shot)**
    > `Establishing wide shot, landscape view of the coastal city of Ensenada at night, the main street "Calle Primera" is devastated, ruined storefronts, broken glass, columns of dark smoke rising into the night sky, the white city hall building in the background is partially burned with carbonized walls, foggy air, moonlight piercing through the smoke, volumetric lighting, epic scale, 8k resolution, cinematic look --ar 16:9`

*   **Prompt 2: Toma Interior de Gran Angular (Interior Wide Shot)**
    > `Wide-angle interior shot of a ransacked municipal office inside the burned city hall, charred wooden desks, broken glass windows, ashes covering the floor, light coming from the window, high detail, dramatic lighting, shot on 24mm lens, realistic, soot-covered walls --ar 16:9`

*   **Prompt 3: Toma de Detalle (Close-up Detail)**
    > `Cinematic close-up of a broken white municipal logo sign, partially burned and soot-stained, resting on wet asphalt, dramatic low-angle shot, moody atmosphere, highly detailed, puddle reflecting police lights --ar 16:9`
